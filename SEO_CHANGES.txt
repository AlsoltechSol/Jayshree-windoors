POST-DEPLOYMENT NOTE

Indexing and rankings cannot be guaranteed by on-site changes. Search engines must
recrawl the corrected signals, and local visibility also depends on a complete,
consistent Google Business Profile/Bing Places listing and genuine external
mentions. Do not add claims, ratings, reviews, service areas, or opening hours to
schema unless they are accurate and also visible to visitors.
